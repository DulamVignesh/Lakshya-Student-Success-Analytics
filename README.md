# Lakshya-Student-Success-Analytics
Power BI dashboard analyzing student performance, dropout risk, and career recommendations using data analytics.

#Problem Explanation

Educational institutions today face several challenges related to student performance monitoring, dropout prevention, and career guidance. Although large amounts of student data such as academic scores, attendance records, skill assessments, and engagement metrics are collected by institutions, this data is often not effectively analyzed to generate meaningful insights.

As a result, educators and administrators find it difficult to identify students who are at risk of dropping out, understand the factors influencing academic performance, and guide students toward suitable career paths. Without proper analysis, it becomes challenging to detect patterns such as low attendance, declining academic scores, or lack of engagement that may indicate potential academic difficulties.

Traditional reporting methods typically present raw data in spreadsheets or static reports, which makes it hard to quickly interpret trends or relationships between different factors affecting student success. Institutions require a visual and interactive system that can transform student data into actionable insights.

To address these challenges, there is a need for a data-driven analytics dashboard that can analyze student performance, attendance, skill levels, and engagement patterns. Such a system can help identify potential risks early, support academic decision-making, and provide insights that assist institutions in improving student success outcomes.

# Lakshya: Student Success Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
Lakshya is a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze student academic performance, skill development, and dropout risk using data analytics.

The project provides insights into student engagement, attendance patterns, and career recommendations to help institutions make data-driven decisions.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Data Analytics
- DAX Measures
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview
- Total Students
- Average Academic Score
- Average Attendance
- High Dropout Risk Students
- Average Skill Score

### 2️⃣ Academic Performance Analysis
- Attendance vs Academic Performance
- Skill Score by Department
- Engagement Level Analysis
- Academic Score Distribution
- Academic Trend by Semester

### 3️⃣ Dropout Risk & Career Insights
- Dropout Risk Distribution
- Dropout Risk by Department
- Dropout Risk by Attendance Group
- Career Recommendation Distribution
- Career Recommendation by Department

## Key Insights
- Students with attendance below 60% show higher dropout risk.
- Computer Science department has the highest student count.
- Higher engagement levels correlate with better academic performance.
